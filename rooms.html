ROOMS







Poolside One-Bedroom Suite

The Poolside One Bedroom Suite is a self-catering, one-bedroom suite with a bedroom, living/dining room and kitchen. It is located in the courtyard area of the hotel. It has a total of 616 square feet (500 square feet of interior living space and 116 square feet of private balcony). The bedroom contains two queen beds, a dresser, flat-screen TV and large closet.







Premium Gulf-Front Suite (King Bed)

Located on the St. Pete beachfront, the Premium Gulf-Front Suite is a self-catering, one-bedroom suite with a separate living/dining room and kitchenette. It has a total of 496 square feet (431 square feet of interior living space and 65 square feet on a private Gulf-front balcony). The bedroom contains one king bed, a dresser, flat-screen TV and a large closet.






Deluxe Gulf-Front Suite

Located on the beachfront, the Deluxe Gulf-Front Suite is a self-catering, one-bedroom suite w/ a bedroom, living, dining room and kitchenette. It has a total of 484 square feet (400 square feet of interior living space and 84 square feet on a private Gulf-front balcony). The bedroom contains two double beds, a dresser, flat-screen TV and large closet.
